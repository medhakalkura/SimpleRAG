# Prompt engineering guide for image generation

Use this document as reference when improving user prompts for text-to-image models.
The content below will be embedded and retrieved by RAG to enhance prompts.

## Default aesthetic: cozy, colorful, warm
- Preferred style: cozy, warm, colorful, inviting, soft, nostalgic.
- Color palette: warm tones, pastels, muted jewel tones, earthy accents, soft gradients.
- Atmosphere: "cozy interior", "warm lighting", "soft glow", "lived-in", "inviting".
- Add: "vibrant but soft colors", "warm color palette", "cozy aesthetic" when the user does not specify otherwise.

## Style and quality keywords
- For photorealistic images: add "photograph, 8k, highly detailed, sharp focus, professional lighting".
- For digital art: "digital art, concept art, vibrant colors, detailed".
- For illustration: "illustration, clean lines, artistic, stylized".
- For cinematic look: "cinematic lighting, dramatic, wide angle, film grain".

## Composition and framing
- Specify aspect ratio when relevant: "16:9", "square", "portrait orientation".
- Use terms like "close-up", "wide shot", "medium shot", "overhead view" for framing.
- "Rule of thirds", "centered composition", "symmetrical" help control layout.

## Mood and atmosphere
- Lighting: "golden hour", "blue hour", "soft diffused light", "warm ambient glow", "moody shadows".
- Weather and environment: "misty", "rainy", "sunny day", "night scene", "foggy".
- Emotional tone: "peaceful", "cozy", "inviting", "nostalgic", "dreamy", "mysterious".
- For cozy scenes: "soft lighting", "warm tones", "layered textiles", "plants", "books", "candles".

## Subject description
- Be specific: "a red vintage car" instead of "a car"; "wooden cabin with chimney" instead of "a house".
- Include material and texture when it matters: "rustic wooden table", "marble floor", "woven basket".
- Cozy details: "plush cushions", "woven blankets", "warm wood", "plants and greenery", "soft textiles", "vintage decor".

## What to avoid in prompts
- Avoid very long run-on sentences; break into clear clauses.
- Avoid contradictory terms (e.g. "bright dark scene").
- For best results with most image models, keep the enhanced prompt under 300 words and focus on visual details.
